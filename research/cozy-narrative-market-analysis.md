**Prepared for:** NorthernStar Studio  
**Date:** March 11, 2026  
**Analyst:** Gabe 📊

---

## Executive Summary

The cozy narrative-driven game market represents a **$973M segment in 2025**, projected to reach **$1.47B by 2032** (6.5% CAGR). This is a mature, growing market with strong player demand for low-stress, emotionally resonant experiences. Premium titles priced **$15-30** with **8-12 hour narratives** are seeing significant growth.

## 7. Market Trends & Gaps

### Emerging Trends:
- **AI-powered personalization** in cozy games
- **Cross-platform progression** expected
- **Wellness/mindfulness** integration
- **Short-form** experiences (2-4 hrs) gaining traction
- **"Digging" mechanics** trending (meditative, tactile)

### Potential Gaps:
- Narrative-driven cozy games with **deeper choices/consequences**
- **Multiplayer cozy** (underserved)
- **Non-farm/craft** cozy mechanics
- **Diverse cultural settings** beyond typical fantasy
